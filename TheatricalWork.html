Backstage Work

Rocky Horror Picture Show

Brian's first experience working in theatre actually came about as a result of a movie. In Spring of 2023, he served as a props runner for the USC Dorothy and Friends Theatre Company when they put on a shadow cast production of the Rocky Horror Picture Show. He was responsible for handling props in the small corner on the wings stage left in USC's now-demolished small black box, the Massman Theatre. Near the end of the showing, he and his counterpart from stage right would also handle deck crew duties, moving tables and chairs as necessary for the performance.

Somewhere Someone is Traveling Furiously Towards You

All students in the USC School of Dramatic Arts, no matter the creative field that they are working towards, are required to take at least one course about the backstage components of theatrical production. They learn the basics of each theatrical field, and crew one of the productions being put on by SDA. In Spring 2024, as part of this course, Brian crewed the SDA MFA New Works Festival, doing Sound Board for Somewhere Someone is Traveling Furiously Towards You, written by SDA MFA Playwriting 3rd year Marilyn Scotland, with direction by Alison De La Cruz. He learned the basics of QLab operation from Sound Designer Alma Reyes-Thomas, and gained some insight into the process of developing sound design for a live theatre setting.

Written Work

About Face: Women, War, and Re-envisioning Troy

The 2023-2024 USC Visions and Voices season featured a pair of events known as About Face: Women, War, and Re-envisioning Troy. In it, participants and audience members were invited to reflect on the way women were perceived and treated in the history of our society, and our university, and how myths of old could take on new meanings in today's world. Brian's submission for the event, Cass's Cake, was one of the short works selected for a staged reading on the night of the first event, with direction by Nico Fife. In the work, Brian touches upon societal tendencies of putting the needs and wants of men above those of their female counterparts.
