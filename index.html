Brian Mendoza

Brian Mendoza is an actor, playwright, and musician living in Los Angeles, CA. He has appeared as an extra in film and television programs such as The Goldbergs, Jarhead, and Veronica Mars, and has performed music for The Masked Singer, Coachella, BottleRock, and live sporting events. He is currently refining his skills at the University of Southern California's School of Dramatic Arts.
